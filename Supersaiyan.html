Super Saiyan 1

How To Acheive

This transformation is the stepping stone into entering further super saiyan levels, this is purely an anger based transformation until the buu saga, where it is revealed that children born after their parents have attained the transformation can turn super saiyan easily. Also in dragonball super, it is revealed that saiyans can tranform by targetting the energy in the back of their spine, as shown by caulifa.

Power Boost

This transformation grants the user with a 50 times boost to their base speed and power at the expense of their stamina.

Super Saiyan God

How to Acheive

This transformation is achieved by 5 saiyans instilling their energy into one other saiyan who is pure in heart - as shown in battle of Gods, this transformation greats the user with God energy, which can not be sensed by anyone besides those who have god ki. As of dragonball Super it has been shown that this transformation can also be attained by a mortal who learns how to access God Ki. In order to access god ki the user must keep all thier ki within their body and not let any slip out while they are raising thier power. Once the user has access to god ki they will have access to Super Saiyan God at free will.

Power Boost

This transformation is harder to calculate what the power boost is per user, but it has been stated to be stronger than all 3 super saiyan levels ( Super saiyan 1 = 50x , Super saiyan 2 = 100x & Super Saiyan 3 = 400x). So Super Saiyan God is more than a 400x boost.

Super Saiyan Blue

How to Achieve

This transformation is achieved in a similar way to the second method of turning into a super saiyan god. As described by Goku to frieza, " Simply you have to turn into a super saiyan god then turn into a super saiyan" This can be seen as quite confusing as the term super saiyan is already in super saiyan god. After the user turns into a super saiyan blue their ki can not be detected by normal mortals, so power levels are out of the question.

Power Boost

This tranformation is hard to calcualte as there has not been a guide on this one also, but in the anime, it was shown during Goku's battle with a fused character - Kefla. When Kefla was in base form she was on par with Goku in Super Saiyan God. Then when Kefla went super saiyan goku turned into super saiyan blue to even the playing field which shows that super saiyan blue is a 50x boost from super saiyan God.
